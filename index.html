About Geeta Polyclinic

A trusted name in healthcare, serving our community with dedication, care, and modern medical expertise.

Geeta Polyclinic has been a cornerstone of healthcare in our community for over two decades. Founded with the vision of providing accessible, quality medical care to every patient, we have grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility trusted by thousands of families.

Our team of experienced doctors and healthcare professionals work together to provide personalized care. We believe every patient deserves to be treated with compassion, respect, and the highest standard of medical excellence.

  • ✅ Experienced and qualified medical professionals
  • 🕐 Timely appointments with minimal waiting time
  • 💊 Attached medical store for all your medicine needs
  • 🧪 Diagnostic tests and health checkup packages
  • 🌿 Holistic and patient-centered care approach

Our Medical Services

From general consultations to specialist care — we cover a wide range of medical services to keep you healthy.

🩺

General Consultation

Expert diagnosis and treatment for common illnesses, fevers, infections, and general health concerns.

❤️

Cardiology

Heart health evaluations, ECG testing, and expert care for cardiovascular conditions.

🦴

Orthopedics

Treatment for bone, joint, and muscle problems including fractures, arthritis, and back pain.

👶

Pediatrics

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents with child-friendly environment.

🧠

Neurology

Diagnosis and management of neurological disorders including migraines, seizures, and more.

Pathology & Lab

Gynecology

Women's health services including prenatal care, routine checkups, and specialist consultations.

Eye Care

Comprehensive vision testing, eye health assessments, and specialist referrals when needed.
