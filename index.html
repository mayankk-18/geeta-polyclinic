About Geeta Polyclinic

Station Club Market Complex, OT Rd, Balasore, Odisha

Geeta Polyclinic has been a cornerstone of healthcare in Balasore for over two decades. Founded with the vision of providing accessible, quality medical care to every patient, we have grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility trusted by thousands of families across the region.

Our team of experienced doctors and healthcare professionals work together to provide personalized care. We believe every patient deserves to be treated with compassion, respect, and the highest standard of medical excellence.
